Disown

What is Disown?

Disown is a forum platform where anyone can create their own forum, their own community, in which every member can communicate, participate and organize in real-time without relying on any other software or platform, thus disowning them.

Disown is essentially a combination of the forum-like style of Reddit, and the real time text or voice chatting system of Discord. This service is primarily used for gamers, but it is available for anyone to use.
